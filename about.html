About Us

TAO GROUP is a conglomerate that consists of 4 companies.
As an entity, we realise that our position affords us the opportunity to improve the environment and the lives of people in the cities we set up shop.

TAO BUILDING AND CONSTRUCTION

We have stayed true to the belief hat true progress is seen in the quality of life of the people around us. We intend to build durable, aesthetically pleasing, and functional houses and business places.
We will always employ modern construcion techniques and equipments in our design and execution process to ensure we give you the best.

TAO INTEGRATED FARMS LIMITED & BURGEON FARMS AND AGRO INDUSTRIES LIMITED

As our society grows, our need to increase provision of sustenance for the populace becomes more important. To tackle this challenge, we have invested in livestock and crop production, employing modern farming practises and machines.

TAOREED LAWAL FOUNDATION

While we believe in the individuality of people, we also understand that not everybody has access to the opportunities and resources that they need.
To address this, TAO Foundation will focus on providing, without any expected reciprocation, basic needs (and sometimes funds) to those in our immediate society who will need it.
We intend to eradicate the present lack, creating an ideal society where every individual has enough to go on to chase their dreams.

Our Vision

A self-sustainable society with productive individuals and a healthy environment.

Our Mission

To build a self-sufficient, collectively productive society.

Our Goal

Advancement in all areas of societal development and through technology
